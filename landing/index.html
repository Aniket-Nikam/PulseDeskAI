FAQ

Common Questions

Here are answers to common questions about PulseDesk's hosting, privacy model, and usage.

Is my data shared with PulseDesk servers?

▼
No. PulseDesk is fully self-hosted. The entire application (database, API endpoints, and dashboards) is deployed inside your own network (on-premise or your private cloud). No activity logs or metadata ever leave your servers.

How do clients sync configuration?

▼
Enrolled devices run our lightweight agent background service. The agent syncs domain blocklists, retrieves screenshot scheduling intervals, and uploads telemetry batches securely via encrypted HTTP endpoints and WebSockets.

Can employees opt out or manage consent?

▼
Yes. PulseDesk includes a dedicated employee portal. Employees can log in to view their own recorded timeline, give or withdraw monitoring consent in compliance with GDPR Article 7, and export their full profile data to satisfy Article 15 requirements.

What are the requirements for self-hosting?

▼
Any server capable of running Docker can host PulseDesk. Our compose stack deploys a FastAPI server, a React static client, and a PostgreSQL database using one unified docker-compose configuration.
